Description of COMP2156

COMP2156 is a course taught at George Brown College and is taught in the 4th semester of the Computer Systems Technician and Computer Systems Technology programs

In the course, you will be introduced to the basic functions of DevOps and the concept of DevOps

There are 10 quizzes, 10 labs, 2 assignments, and 2 exams

The best 8 quizzes will be the ones that are counted

8 of the 10 labs have to be completed
